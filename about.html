About

Our History:

Lucci’s Boating began as a small, family-run operation along the banks of the Hudson River in Hudson, New York. Founded by a local boating enthusiast with a passion for life on the water, the company started with just a single small motorboat and a simple idea: to give people an easy and memorable way to experience the Hudson. In the early days, most customers were locals looking for weekend fishing trips or short scenic rides, and word quickly spread about the welcoming service and personal attention that set the company apart.

As interest in the Hudson River grew as both a tourism destination and a recreational boating hub, Lucci’s Boating expanded its offerings. The fleet grew to include a variety of rental boats suited for fishing, sightseeing, and group outings, along with guided tours highlighting the river’s scenic landscapes and historic waterfront towns. During this time, the company also began hosting small fishing events, which eventually developed into organized tournaments that attracted anglers from across the region. This growth transformed Lucci’s Boating from a local rental service into a recognized part of the Hudson Valley boating community.

Today, Lucci’s Boating is known for combining recreation, education, and competition on one of New York’s most iconic waterways. While the company has grown significantly since its early days, it still maintains its original focus on providing a safe, approachable, and enjoyable experience for every guest. Whether customers are learning to navigate the river for the first time, joining a guided tour, or competing in a fishing tournament, Lucci’s Boating continues to emphasize a strong connection to the Hudson River and the community that surrounds it.

Our Mission

Lucci’s Boating exists to make the Hudson River accessible, enjoyable, and safe for everyone—from first-time boaters to experienced anglers. Our mission is to provide high-quality boat rentals, guided tours, and fishing experiences that allow customers to confidently explore one of New York’s most iconic waterways. We believe that time on the water should be both exciting and stress-free, which is why we focus on clear instruction, reliable equipment, and a welcoming environment for all skill levels.

Beyond recreation, our mission is rooted in education and respect for the Hudson River itself. We aim to help every guest understand the importance of safe boating practices, awareness of local conditions, and appreciation for the river’s unique ecosystem. By combining fun, learning, and environmental responsibility, Lucci’s Boating strives to create experiences that not only leave lasting memories but also encourage a deeper connection to the water and the surrounding Hudson Valley community.
