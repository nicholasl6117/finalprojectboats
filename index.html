Lucci's Boating

Under Construction

This will be a site for a fictional company that focuses on boating. They offer boat rentals as well as boat tours on the Hudson River. Below is a list of different things that will be included in the company's website.

  • Home Page: Provides an overview of what the company is and what it does.
  • Education Page: Provides pictures and descriptions of the different types of boats that the company owns and uses for it's services.
  • Services Page: Provides a more in-depth description of the services offered by the company; includes what exactly the rented boats can be used for and what exactly the boat tours include. May discuss pricing, etc.
  • About Section: Provides a brief history of the company and what it's values are, as well as contact information.

Additional features in the website will include:

  • Media queries to adjust to different size screens
  • A footer with an automatically-updating copyright year
  • Proper formatting that is consistent across the pages
  • A "back to top" button at the bottom of the webpages
  • Navigation bar, aside sections, etc
